Academic Projects

Field-based Stool Diagnostic Device for Soil-Transmitted Helminths (STH) | UC Berkeley | Fall 2024

  • Developed a novel stool sample processing method for concentration of STH eggs on custom acrylic inserts.
  • Created a machine learning algorithm for classification of STH eggs from stool samples on NTDscope.
  • Designed user observation survey and field study outline for validation in Bangladesh.
  • Drafted an NIH R21 proposal for the field study.

Droplet Microfluidics-Based Point-of-Care Diagnostic for Tuberculosis | UC Berkeley | Fall 2019

  • Developed a bead-based assay for antigen capture and subsequent ELISA on beads.
  • Demonstrated ELISA on beads using synthetic target antigen.
  • Designed a droplet generator for femtoliter-sized droplets and calculated parameters for single-molecule ELISA.
  • Determined fluorescence detector parameters for signal measurement from droplets.

Liposome-Based Biosensor for Point-of-Care Detection of Phospholipase A2 in Lateral Flow Device | UIUC | Fall 2016

  • Reviewed and analyzed liposome-based POC diagnostic devices for Phospholipase A2 detection.
  • Explored its potential as a biomarker for pancreatitis, atherosclerosis, sepsis, prostate cancer, and lung cancer.

Identification of an Unknown Bacteria from Raw Sequence Reads | UIUC | Spring 2016

  • Processed raw sequence reads into contigs and scaffolds using PERL and R.
  • Assembled complete genome and annotated potential genes using bioinformatics tools.
  • Determined phylogenetic relationship using BLASTp searches against NCBI GenBank.

Point-of-Care Diagnostic X-ray Machine for Radiographic Assessment of Cervical Spine | UIUC | Fall 2015

  • Proposed and designed a portable x-ray imaging device for emergency medical services.
  • Enabled rapid and accurate cervical spine assessment in field settings.

Magnetic Nanoparticle-Conjugated Antibody for Treatment of Rheumatoid Arthritis | UIUC | Fall 2014

  • Modeled an antibody-conjugated magnetic nanoparticle for targeted therapy.
  • Designed monoclonal TNF-specific antibodies for efficient delivery to affected joints.
